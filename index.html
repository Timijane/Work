About Prophet Samuel Adegboyega

Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega (born 22 June 1945) is a Nigerian pastor, prophet, philanthropist, and Christian leader who serves as the Founder, President, and First General Overseer of the Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC) Worldwide, headquartered in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. He is widely known for his commitment to prayer, holiness, prophetic ministry, and the transformative power of the Holy Spirit. Beyond his ecclesiastical work, Adegboyega is the founder of Focus for the Future (FFF), a philanthropic initiative dedicated to youth empowerment, educational advancement, and community development. Through FFF, hundreds of students receive scholarships annually, while numerous young people benefit from mentorship, leadership training, and financial support designed to help them realize their potential and contribute meaningfully to society. Over several decades of ministry and humanitarian service, he has earned recognition as a spiritual mentor, educator, and advocate for the advancement of future generations.

Timing Is Critical

Life is too short for you to live striving to force doors of opportunity to open, trying to convince someone to like you and working hard to win their approval, straining to manipulate a situation so you can win the upper hand. That’s going to wear you out and waste your time. David recognized that his times were in God’s hands. He was saying, “I’m not going to be frustrated by delays, or sour when it’s not working out the way I want, or discouraged because I’m not as far along as I thought I’d be. My God is in control.”

Timing is critical. You have to realize that you can do the right thing, but if it’s not the right time, it won’t work out. When it’s not the right time, no matter how hard you try, the door is not going to open. When you don’t feel a peace about it, when there is a sense of unrest in your spirit, you can’t make the wrong time into the right time...

About the Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC)

The Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC) Worldwide is a Pentecostal Christian ministry founded by Prophet Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega and headquartered in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. The church is committed to proclaiming the message of salvation through Jesus Christ, promoting holiness, prayer, evangelism, prophetic ministry, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Over the years, SPAC has expanded across Nigeria with branches in cities including Ibadan, Lagos, and Abeokuta, as well as an international presence in the United Kingdom through SPAC Nxtion Family, led by Pastor Tobi Adegboyega. Beyond its spiritual mission, the church is actively involved in youth empowerment, scholarships, welfare support, and community development initiatives aimed at transforming lives and raising future leaders.
