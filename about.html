Official Biography

Prophet Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega

From humble beginnings in Gboyega Village to a global mantle of leadership and philanthropy.

A Life Defined by Divine Purpose

Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega (born 22 June 1945) is a Nigerian pastor, prophet, philanthropist, and Christian leader who serves as the Founder, President, and First General Overseer of the Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC) Worldwide, headquartered in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. He is widely known for his commitment to prayer, holiness, prophetic ministry, and the transformative power of the Holy Spirit.

The Early Struggle

Born in Gboyega Village, Ogun State, to Adegboyega Lukotun Olowe, a farmer and community founder, and Egbetoto Ilori Adegboyega, a trader, Samuel's early years were shaped by the realities of rural poverty. Despite the hardship, his mother’s unwavering determination ensured he received an education. His academic brilliance was evident early on; he earned double promotions in primary school and eventually graduated from Lisabi Grammar School in 1964 with a prestigious Grade One result.

"His professional career as a Chartered Secretary was marked by rare dedication, rising to become a Partner at Z.O. Ososanya & Co—a testament to the integrity that would later define his ministry."

The Call to Ministry

Though raised amidst traditional influences, Samuel’s path shifted in 1960 upon his baptism. A pivotal encounter in 1967 at Saint Paul’s Apostolic Church ignited a lifelong covenant of holiness. By 1978, responding to a divine directive following a major revival, he pioneered the Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC). His commitment was total; he famously sold his own residential property to fund the construction of the church’s headquarters, proving his sacrifice preceded his authority.

FFF: A Philanthropic Legacy

Beyond the pulpit, Adegboyega’s vision is anchored in the "Focus for the Future" (FFF) initiative. Recognizing that the youth are the custodians of tomorrow, he established FFF to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity. Through annual scholarships, mentorship programs, and financial welfare for widows and entrepreneurs, he has transformed thousands of lives. His legacy is not merely in the structures he built, but in the leaders he has raised and the families he has supported through the storms of life.
