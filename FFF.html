Focus for the Future (FFF)

Empowering Today’s Youth. Transforming Tomorrow’s World.

About the Initiative

Focus for the Future (FFF) is a humanitarian, educational, and youth empowerment initiative founded by Prophet Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega. Established from a deep passion for education, leadership development, and social transformation, the foundation is committed to helping young people overcome barriers, discover their potential, and build meaningful futures.

The vision behind FFF is rooted in the personal life journey of its founder. Having experienced severe poverty and financial hardship during his childhood, Prophet Adegboyega understands firsthand the challenges that many young people face in pursuing education and achieving their dreams. Through determination, faith, hard work, and the support of a few compassionate individuals, he was able to rise above those circumstances and build a life of purpose and impact. Focus for the Future was established to extend similar opportunities to others, ensuring that financial limitations do not prevent talented and determined young people from reaching their full potential.

Since its inception, FFF has become a platform of hope and opportunity for countless students, families, and communities. The foundation has supported hundreds of students through scholarship programmes, educational assistance, mentorship initiatives, and leadership development opportunities. By investing in education and human capital, FFF seeks to create lasting change that extends beyond individual beneficiaries to entire families and communities.

At Focus for the Future, we believe that every young person possesses unique gifts, talents, and abilities capable of making a positive contribution to society. However, many gifted individuals are denied opportunities because of economic challenges, lack of guidance, or limited access to resources. Our mission is to bridge that gap by providing practical support, encouragement, and opportunities that enable young people to succeed academically, professionally, and personally.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of educated, empowered, responsible, and purpose-driven leaders who will positively transform their communities, nations, and the world.

Our Mission

To provide educational opportunities, scholarships, mentorship, leadership development, and community support programmes that help young people discover their potential and achieve their aspirations.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We encourage the pursuit of academic, professional, and personal excellence in every area of life.
  • Integrity: We promote honesty, accountability, responsibility, and ethical leadership.
  • Service: We believe true leadership is demonstrated through service to others and commitment to community development.
  • Empowerment: We are committed to equipping individuals with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities they need to succeed.
  • Compassion: We seek to uplift the disadvantaged and provide hope where it is most needed.

What We Do

Educational Scholarships

Education remains one of the most effective tools for breaking the cycle of poverty. Through our scholarship programmes, FFF provides financial assistance to deserving students from various backgrounds.

Student Support Initiatives

Beyond scholarships, the foundation provides educational materials, learning resources, counselling support, and other forms of assistance.

Youth Empowerment Programmes

FFF organises programmes designed to equip young people with practical life skills, leadership abilities, and entrepreneurial knowledge.

Mentorship and Leadership Development

The foundation connects young people with experienced mentors, professionals, educators, and leaders who provide guidance and encouragement.

Our Founder

Focus for the Future was founded by Prophet Samuel Ajayi Adegboyega, Founder, President, and First General Overseer of the Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church (SPAC) Worldwide. Inspired by his own journey from rural poverty to becoming a respected leader, he established FFF as a vehicle for creating opportunities for future generations.
